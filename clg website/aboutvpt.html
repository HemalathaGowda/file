WELCOME TO VIVEKANANDA POLYTECHNIC

Introduction:

An institution established by Vivekananda VidyaVardhaka Sangha Puttur (R), formely known as Puttur Education Society (Regd) Puttur, D.K. during 1986-87 as per the approval given by the Government of Karnataka vide Order No. ED 39 MPI 86 dated 18-04-1986, Vivekananda Polytechnic has been consistently rendering quality education in Engineering and Technology for the last 25 years.

All through these years the Polytechnic has grown leaps and bounds. Every passing year has added to the magnitude and status of the Polytechnic with real up gradation and growth.

Location:

Vivekananda Polytechnic is located at Nehru Nagar, Puttur D.K. about 3 kms from Puttur Town along Mercara –Mangalore State Highway. The campus where the Polytechnic is situated in very ideal surroundings. The vast campus impresses every one by its scenic beauty, Majestic valleys around and the surrounding greenery. Being away from the din of the Town it is free from the afflictions of city life, and makes an ideal place for academic pursuits.

Aims and Objectives

Vivekananda Polytechnic has been established at Puttur in order to meet the long felt need of the people of area for an Institution imparting technical education. Our mission is to achieve excellence in technical education through discipline. The Polytechnic aims at providing a high standard of education in the instructional areas selected. The courses being offered are in keeping with the needs of the area and also the prospective opportunities.

The polytechnic will strive to provide all the facilities needed by the students to complete their studies in a systematic way. Adequate industrial orientation,training and our institution provides a platform for the placement of the students in different leading organizations and helps to prepare them for competitive world.

The Courses:

The Polytechnic offers the following 3 year Diploma courses:

  1. Computer science and Engineering
  2. Mechanical Engineering
  3. Civil Engineering
  4. Automobile Engineering
  5. Electronicss and Communication Engineering
