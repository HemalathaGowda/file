COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

Computers have found their way into almost all walks of life. Diverse area like work processing, financial accounting, travel reservation, Desktop publishing, Computer aided design, Computer aided manufacturing, the list is practically endless. Computer technocrats apply the principles and techniques of Computer Science, Engineering and Mathematical analysis to the design, development, testing and evaluation of the software and systems.

The development in the field of communication and internet technology has resulted in a rising demand for Technocrats in the field of Computer Science & information Technology. The following laboratory facilities are available in Computer Science department.

  1. Web Technology Lab
  2. Data Structure Lab
  3. PC Hardware Lab
  4. Network Administration Lab
  5. Visual Basic, Linux, Java & ASP. Net Lab
